Conservation Co. reservation park.

One of the main ways to conserve wildlife is to protect wildlife habitats and that is what ConservatioCo reservation park is all about.

Saving the Wildlife

In some cases, the habitats for certain species have been destroyed to the point where the animals cannot survive in the wild. Fortunately, zoos have maintained the species and helped them to grow their population while in captivity

Saving Endangered species

The bald eagle was nearly wiped out by the pesticide DDT. After the DDT was banned, the population increased and was removed from endangered list in 2007.

WHAT WE DO?

ConservationCo is about protecting wild species and their habitats in order to prevent species from going extinct. Major threats to wildlife include habitat destruction/degradation/fragmentation, overexploitation, poaching, pollution and climate change. An increasing number of ecosystems on earth containing endangered species are disappearing.To address this issues, there have been both national and international governmental efforts to preserve Earth's wildlife.

ConservationCo is doing it's part to preserve wildlife by creating reservation parks, establishing zoos and tracking the population of endangered species to know when they are shrinking or growing and approprite measures are taken to ensure their continous survival.
